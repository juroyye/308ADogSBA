All of our dogs are brought in from the street!

Fido

Are you looking for a little buddy with a big personality? Meet Fido, our pint-sized powerhouse with an irresistible charm! Fido is an Affenpinscher—a unique toy breed famous for their playful, "monkey-like" expressions and boundless spirit. Weighing in at around 10 pounds but full of courage, Fido is a loyal friend who would love nothing more than to find a forever home with someone ready to appreciate his spunky attitude and affectionate heart.

Lucy

Introducing Lucy, a strikingly beautiful Akita with a heart as big as her smile! Lucy is the kind of dog who makes an unforgettable impression—she’s intelligent, calm, and fiercely loyal to those she loves. With her plush double coat and noble stance, Lucy truly embodies the dignity of her breed. But beneath that majestic appearance lies a gentle, affectionate soul looking for a family to call her own.

Rook

Looking for a best friend who’s as graceful as they are affectionate? Say hello to Rook, a sleek and loving Whippet who’s ready to race right into your heart! With his slim build and athletic frame, Rook is an elegant sight to behold, but don’t let his racing-dog looks fool you—this sweet boy is a certified couch snuggler at heart. Whippets are known for their gentle nature, and Rook is no exception. He’s soft-hearted, easygoing, and adores curling up beside his favorite humans, especially after a good sprint in the yard.
